How This Forecast Works

This forecast uses Linear Regression Machine Learning to analyze historical sales trends and project future performance. The model identifies patterns in monthly sales data and category-specific trends, then extrapolates these patterns to predict the next 6 months.

Key Features:

  • Overall Trend: Analyzes if sales are generally increasing or decreasing
  • Category Analysis: Forecasts per-category growth rates to identify opportunities and risks
  • Confidence Score: Indicates prediction reliability based on historical data consistency
  • Growth Rate: Shows monthly percentage change for each category

Note: Predictions are based on historical trends. External factors like market changes, promotions, or seasonality should be considered when making business decisions.
